Next.js gives me a strong default structure for routing, layouts, metadata, image optimization, and deployment.

## Why it helps

For student projects, this means I spend less time wiring infrastructure and more time improving the actual product:

- App Router keeps route structure clean.
- Server components help keep pages fast.
- Metadata tools make SEO easier.
- Vercel deployment is straightforward.

It also gives projects a more professional feel because the app structure scales better than a single large frontend file.
